The Natural Majesty of the Hisui Region

Discover the Hisui region, Jubilife Village, and the characters of Pokémon Legends: Arceus.

Your adventure is set in the expansive natural majesty of the Hisui region,
in an age long ago when it was rare for people and Pokémon to live in close harmony.
In time, this land of Hisui will come to be called Sinnoh—a region you may know well.

In Pokémon Legends: Arceus, the Hisui region offers diverse areas for exploration. The Obsidian Fieldlands are lush and
ideal for early adventures. The Crimson Mirelands feature challenging swamps, while the Cobalt Coastlands provide
coastal scenery and aquatic Pokémon. The Coronet Highlands present rugged, mountainous terrain, and the Alabaster Icelands
are harsh and snowy with ice-type Pokémon. Each unique area enhances the game's immersive experience.

Frenzy Pokemon

In Pokémon Legends: Arceus, frenzied Noble Pokémon become difficult to control due to a strange phenomenon. As a member of the Survey Corps, you must calm them using balms made from their favorite foods. Dodge their powerful attacks and throw balms when there's an opening. Defeating them in Pokémon battles will daze them, giving you a chance to hit them with more balms and quell their frenzy.

The Young Leaders of the Diamond and Pearl Clan

Adaman leads the Diamond Clan and is partnered with Leafeon. He is a daring young man who dislikes wasting time and prefers to act quickly without worrying about details. This impulsive approach often puts him at odds with Irida, the leader of the Pearl Clan, leading to frequent conflicts between them.

Adaman

Irida leads the Pearl Clan with Glaceon as her partner Pokémon. She values courage, essential for facing the challenges of the vast Hisui region. Initially suspicious of newcomers, Irida's trust is earned through their assistance in calming the frenzied noble Pokémon, highlighting her evolving relationship with new allies.

Irida

The Ginkgo Guild: Merchants of the Hisui Region

Volo, one of the Ginkgo Guild’s merchants, is often to be found wandering the Hisui region. He has a tendency to cross paths with you no matter where you travel. He’s got a boundless sense of curiosity and is always interested in unusual finds and unusual people.
